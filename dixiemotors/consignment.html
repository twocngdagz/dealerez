Consignment Program

Showroom Sales & Leasing Consignment Program



Sell your Luxury, Classic, or Exotic vehicle quickly and easily by allowing Showroom Sales & Leasing to do all the work for you. No more interrupting phone calls, strangers to your home, or missed appointments. We will sell you car and then write you a check.
It is that easy!

Consignment Services Include:


  • 12,000 sq ft indoor, carpeted, air-conditioned, and well lit showroom
  • Dusted and hand cleaned routinely
  • Online Postings with Cars.com, Hemmings.com, Craigslist.com & Others with full a description and many color photos
  • Heavy Foot traffic throughout week & week-ends
  • Competitive Financing & Leasing available to buyers
  • Professional Detail Available (at Additional Cost)
  • All Sales Transaction, DMV, and Lien-Holder paperwork
    completed by Showroom Sales & Leasing

Broker Fee Schedule
